People of PDD     2025

An interactive website that invites the public to craft avatars from artworks by artists with disabilities. These creations form a collective digital mural and later animate a 132-metre wall through AR experiences. Blending digital play with community art-making, the project celebrates the people who shape Punggol Digital District.
